Privacy Policy for stats-backend.atl.dev

This Privacy Policy governs the collection, use, and sharing of personal information by stats-backend.atl.dev, a Discord bot developed by All Things Linux. By using stats-backend.atl.dev, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Definitions

“Users” is defined as someone who has opted in using the command “s$optin”.
“Data” is defined as anything in our SQL database.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information about users:

  • Ping counts between users
  • Discord user IDs
  • Discord avatar URLs
  • Discord display names
  • Discord usernames

How We Use Your Information

This data is used to generate the graph file at https://stats-backend.atl.dev/data/graph.gexf. All data we collect from users is publicly accessible in the graph file.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide stats-backend.atl.dev's services or as required by law. We will delete your information upon your request or when it is no longer needed.

Data can be deleted by opting out with “s$optout” or by leaving the Discord server. Upon doing this all stored data is immediately deleted from our database.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no security measure is perfect, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and we will post the updated policy on our website. Your continued use of stats-backend.atl.dev after we make changes to this policy indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@allthingslinux.org.

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of March 8, 2025.
